PCA Results for the Lower White River

Major Results:
  • 4 major groups:
    • → pH
    → E. coli & Temp
    → Copper, Cadmium, Chromium, Nickel, Arsenic
    → Mercury, DO, TDS, Barium, SpCond, Hardness, Chloride, SO42-
  • As a whole, the data points within each group are clustered together. However, the data points between each group are also clustered together. This means we are seeing much overlap between data points and it is hard to distinguish them and associate them with the vectors.
  • What this means in terms of water quality is that the measured pollutants at different points along the Lower White River are relatively similar.
    • → Overall, the Lower White River is associated with heavy metal and E. coli pollution.
  • There is one exception, and that is Lick Creek where there is a correlation with pH.
